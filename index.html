Welcome

I am Htet Arkar Soe, a GIS and Remote Sensing specialist and MSc student in Environmental Science at Chiang Mai University. This website shares my academic journey, research workflow, geospatial projects, publications, training materials, and learning resources.

My work focuses on using geospatial technology to support agriculture, environmental monitoring, land resource management, and community-based mapping.

Current Research

Seasonal Crop and Crop Rotation Mapping in the Chiang Mai–Lamphun Basin using Sentinel-1, Sentinel-2, field validation data, and Satellite Embedding features.
